Recruiting Undergraduate Research Assistants!

I will be recruiting highly motivated, inquisitive, and self-driven undergraduate research assistants (RAs) to start in the spring 2020. This position is ideal for students who are interested in applying to graduate programs related Psychology, Cognitive Science, Neuroscience, Computer Science, or Statistics/Math. RAs will gain experiences with many aspects of the research process including 1) recruiting and testing human subjects, 2) assisting with the design of human behavioral experiments, 3) using a computer programming language (Python), and 4) analyzing data. RAs will work directly with me, and will have the flexibility to develop studies to test their own research questions and opportunities to apply for funding.

Preferred qualifications include:

  • Deep interest in learning about social cognitive and affective neuroscience research (and excited about the empirical work listed here or here)
  • Eagerness to design and conduct psychological experiments
  • Exposure to one or more programming languages (or demonstrate the ability to learn one)
  • Satisfactory completion of PSYC 002 (Research Methods and Statistics) or an equivalent undergraduate statistics course

Please find the application information at this link, which will include a brief Python coding challenge to gauge problem-solving/critical-thinking.
